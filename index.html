First recipe (Apple Pie)

  • Time: 55 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Apples 4
Baking powder Half a packet
Flour 180g
Eggs 2
Brown sugar 100g
Milk 175ml
Berries Some (Decore)
Apricot jam 1 jar
Butter and flour Some (For the mould)

Elaboration

Step 1: Put the flour, the baking powder, the eggs, the sugar, the apples (peeled and cutted in cubes) and the milk in the mixer, and crush it untill it become a homogenous dough.

Step 2: Now pour the mixture into a mold spreaded with butter and flour.

Step 3: Peel the 2 others apples, cut them in leaves and cover the surface of the pie.

Step 4: Bake the pie at 180ºC for 45 minutes.

Step 5: Wait untill the pie gets cold, take it out of the mold, and add a little bit of the apricot jam in the surface

Step 6: Finaly decore it with some of the berries and serve it

Source: hogarmania/applepie

Second recipe (Cheesecake)

  • Time: 55 minutes
  • Price per serve: 0,7$
Ingredients Quantity
Cream cheese 350g
Eggs (XL) 2
Sugar 100g
Wheat flour 40g
Cream (35%GM) 175g

Elaboration

Step 1: Take a mold about 20 X 20 cm, grease it with some butter and put some parchment paper.

Step 2: Preheat the oven at 200 ºC with heat in both sides.

Step 3: Add all the ingredients in a bowl. It´s important that the cream cheese is at natural temperature to work with it easier.

Step 4: Mix all the mixture with your hands or a hand rod untill all of the lumps disappear.

Step 5: Pour all the mixture into the mold and place it into the oven at 200 ºC for 45 minutes with heat up and down.

Step 6: Finaly take the mold out of the oven and wait untill it gets cold.

Source: recetasderechupete/cheesecake
